Smashed Cheeseburger Recipe

Description:

This recipe brings me back to freshman year, where the "kitchen" I had access to was very limited, and a floor below my dorm room. Trying to save money, I would cook from time to time, and this was a staple dish. Smashed Cheeseburgers. You can prepare them lightning quick, their delicious, and minimal dishes to clean.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces of ground beef
  • A slice of American cheese
  • A hamburger bun
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Desired condiments and toppings
